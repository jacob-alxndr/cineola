CINEOLA is a platform for Latin American stories, connecting audiences with diverse representations of Latinoamérica on film.


The festival is co-presented by the Bay Area Video Coalition and raises funds for CARECEN SF, a non-profit responding to the needs, rights and aspirations of Latino, immigrant, and under-resourced families in the San Francisco Bay Area. Proceeds from the 2020 online edition will also support local venues The Roxie and Artists’ Television Access during the COVID-19 pandemic.
